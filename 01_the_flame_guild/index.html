🔥Campaign: The Right to Rest (And Respawn)

We are tired of being tired. Mages, smiths, engineers, and potion brewers are working long hours without proper breaks. We are not asking for magic - just a moment to eat our stew in peace.

Our Main Campaign Goals

  • 1. The 4-Hour Rule - Every worker gets a break every 4 hours. Even during dragon sieges.
  • 2. Universal Bedroll Access - Free, flameproof bedrolls for all guild-certified workers.
  • 3. Respawn Leave - If you're resurrected due to unsafe orking conditions, you get the day off. No arguments.
